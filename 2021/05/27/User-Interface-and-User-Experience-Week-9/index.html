Define is the second phase in design thinking process.

A clear and short summary of issues that must be resolved and used to keep track of the team at the start of the project.

Three main parts:

  1. User
  2. Needs
  3. Goals

the GOALS is to realize the NEEDS from the USERS.

27/5/2021

Crazy8:

This is a simple process of shrinking the paper into 8 equal segments from everyone having an A4 size paper with a Sharpie. It can be named Crazy8 because the whole process must be completed in less than 8 minutes.
You don’t have to think too much about just spilling ideas where you have to spend 1 minute on each segment sketching your ideas or UI.

80/20 principle:
The idea behind this technique is that 80% of the effects of any given process come from 20% of the effort put into it.

  • 80% of your users use 20% of your features
  • 20% of the code causes 80% of the errors
